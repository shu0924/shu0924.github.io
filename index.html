  • Research assistant, AIST, Japan                                                       2015 ~ 2017

    LncRNA is short for long-non-coding RNA, more than 200 nucleotides, which plays important role in the transcript. Cancer is one of the most morbidity and mortality in the world and it is believed that there is a relationship between lncRNA and cancer. Based on the correlation, t-test, multiple-test, and linear-regression, 3146 cancer-associated lncRNA had been discovered by up-regulating or down-regulating the progression of cancer. It revealed that the same lncRNA can induce cancer in a different way and different lncRNA can induce the same cancer.
